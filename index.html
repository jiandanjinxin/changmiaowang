个人简介 [Top]

Changmiao Wang is now an associate professor at Shenzhen Research Institure of Big Data. He obtained Ph.D. degree in pattern recognition and intelligent system from Shenzhen Institutes of Advanced Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2018,
Supervised by Prof. Qingmao Hu、 Prof. Fucang Jia. He was a postdoctoral researcher at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen, from 1/9/2018 to 31/8/2020, where he worked with Prof. Shuguang Cui and Prof. Xiaoguang Han.
