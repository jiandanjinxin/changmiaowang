Short Bio [Top]

Changmiao Wang is an associate professor at the Shenzhen Research Institute of Big Data. He obtained his Ph.D. degree in Pattern Recognition and Intelligent Systems from the Shenzhen Institutes of Advanced Technology, Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2018, supervised by Prof. Qingmao Hu and Prof. Fucang Jia. He was a postdoctoral researcher at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen, from 1/9/2018 to 31/8/2020, where he worked with Prof. Shuguang Cui and Prof. Xiaoguang Han. His research interests include computer vision, medical image analysis, and generative AI applications. He has published nearly 80 papers in top journals and conferences (e.g., JBHI, Neural Networks, ESWA, IEEE TCE, IEEE TAI, IEEE TGRS, KBS, Applied Soft Computing, IEEE/ACM TCBB, BIB, CIBM, ACM MM, MICCAI, BIBM, ISBI).
